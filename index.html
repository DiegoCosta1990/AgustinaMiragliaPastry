About me


Me and my background


Hello there! First of all, thank you so much to visit my page. (Hope you like it!).


I´m Agustina Miraglia. I´m 28 years old and I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina but, nowadays, I live in Alicante, Spain.


I´ve studied in the Argentinian Institute of Gastronomy (IAG) where I recieved my Chef diploma.


My heart takes the decision for me. My preference for the beauty of cakes, sweet decorations, cupcakes, macarons and many more, makes me a better pastry chef everyday. I love learning new aproaches and new flavours to test it and to test me in the daily basis. I love doing things the best I can. I´m kind of perfectionist but not at the point to be my own obstacle. I think that it is the way to improve myself. In fact, I have had some jobs where the quality was not the priority, so, I finally quit and I started my own pastry called Whisk Wishes. My entrepreneurship has given me expierience in all the pastry environment: Finance, clients, the products itself, packaging, selling, social media, etc.


I´ve moved to the beautiful Alicante, Spain with my husband to a fresh start. If you are interested in having me in your pastry team. No doubt it any second. Contact me. Thank you for your time.
